Video Game Project


Description:

Our group was tasked to create an educational video game. We decided to create a math game for 7+. In this game, players will be able to:
- Players will solve mathematical equations using different methods
- Players will earn coins which they can use to purchase cosmetic items to personalize their game experience.



Design and Production Process:

1. Brainstormed various game ideas suitable for all ages.
2. Conducted research on popular games
3. Designed pixelated prototypes of the game.



Reflection:

After doing this project, I learned about:

- The significance of teamwork
- The need for researching games that appeal to a range of age groups


If I could do things differently, I would:

- Look into different games to create

- Discover different games to potentially create
